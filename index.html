Donald Knuth

Donald Knuth was born on January 10th, 1938. He is still alive and well, currently age 84!

Knuth is most famous for writing the series of books called The Art of Computer Programming. In the many volumes of The Art of Computer Programming, Knuth presents the reader with many programming algorithms that are still used today.
